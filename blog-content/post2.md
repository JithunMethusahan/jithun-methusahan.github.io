Running AI models locally means executing large language models directly on your own hardware like Ollama or LM Studio, ensuring total privacy without sending data to external APIs. Top open-weight model families include Qwen3, Gemma 4, and Llama 3.3.Top Local Model FamiliesQwen3 / Qwen3.5: Outstanding multilingual capability, coding prowess, and reasoning with permissive Apache 2.0 licenses in sizes ranging from small edge variants to massive mixture-of-experts architectures.Gemma 4: Google's compact high-performance models (such as the 12B dense variant) featuring native audio-visual support and running smoothly on standard consumer hardware.Llama 3.3: Meta's highly popular ecosystem providing robust community fine-tunes and broad framework support across 8B and 70B parameter configurations.
